# Brand Assets

Third-party provider marks in the Zigns app should be local files sourced from official or official-hosted brand resources, not inline approximations. Keep generic UI icons separate from brand marks. Do not redraw these by hand unless the source is an official SVG snippet and the geometry is preserved.
